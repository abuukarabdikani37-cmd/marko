About Our School

Merca Primary & Secondary School (MPSS) is dedicated to providing quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and strong moral values. Our goal is to create a safe and inspiring learning environment where every student can achieve academic excellence and personal growth.


Through experienced teachers, modern teaching methods, and a commitment to student success, MPSS prepares learners to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Principal's Message

Welcome to MPSS


Kusoo dhowaada Dugsiga Hoose iyo Sare ee Merca (MPSS). Mudane Mohamed Xasan Hooleyste, oo ah maamulaha dugsiga, wuxuu si weyn ugu heellan yahay horumarinta tayada waxbarashada, kobcinta anshaxa ardayda, iyo dhisidda jawi waxbarasho oo dhiirrigeliya guusha. Wuxuu aaminsan yahay in waxbarashadu tahay furaha guusha, isla markaana arday kasta uu leeyahay awood uu ku gaari karo himilooyinkiisa haddii la siiyo fursad iyo hagid wanaagsan.
